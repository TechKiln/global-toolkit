Since TechKenyans is a community around the idea that *Technology is a toolkit to enable you to go about your life efficiently and help you do your best work*, I decided to create this live list of technological tools.

The list cuts across a number of disciplines and will highlight if they are free and when they are not telling you how much it demands and worthy alternatives. [PRs are open](https://github.com/techKenyans/global-toolkit/edit/master/README.md) to give feedback and propose suggestions.
